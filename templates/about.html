Hello, world!

This app enables investors to enter the Ticker of Stocks and Investment funds to check the company ESG scores.

It encourages people to democratize decarbonization!

This project uses the popular open source library developed by Ran Aroussi to download data from 'Yahoo Finance' API 'yfinance'

According to the library - After November 2019 the rating changed to a risk rating. A high ESG score signaled that a company has high ESG risk, meaning that a more sustainable company has a low ESG score.

What is ESG?

Environmental, social, and corporate governance 'ESG' is a strategic framework for identifying, assessing, and addressing organizational objectives and activities ranging from the company's carbon footprint and commitment to sustainability, to its workplace culture and commitment to diversity.
